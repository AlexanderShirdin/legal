Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Pick2Pay (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

Information You Provide

The App is designed to work offline. Data you enter (such as PCS values, rates, goals, penalties, and shift selections) is stored locally on your device to provide the App’s functionality.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account. The App does not ask for your name, phone number, or other direct identifiers. We do not knowingly collect personal information through the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on standard platform services (such as an app store or your device operating system) which may collect information under their own policies. We do not control those services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share the data you enter in the App with third parties because it is stored locally on your device.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and you are responsible for securing access to your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted on this page.
